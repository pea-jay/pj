About Me

I'm Priyanshi Jain, an AI leader with expertise in healthcare. I am the Founder of PrplCrcl, an exclusive community for women's health. I am also serving as AI Scientist in the founding team at RadiantGraph, building voice agents and AI platforms for health plans and services. Previously Lead ML Scientist at Gabbi, where I developed AI solutions for early breast cancer detection with a 47% performance improvement over standard care.

Previously, I improved communication for billions of users at Meta and led ML recommender systems at Teladoc Health that achieved a 28% lift in engagement with published clinical outcomes. My work spans building AI platform with machine learning, deep learning, LLMs for healthcare and clinical applications.

I hold a Master's degree from Carnegie Mellon University and bring a proven track record of quantifiable impact across organizations - from increasing engagement by double digits to negotiating data acquisition costs down by 92%.
