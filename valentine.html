Will you be my Valentine?

Roses are dead
days are blue
Dyed my hair red
so I could feel something new
Roses are red
they bid me adieu
Why do they shed,
lose their hue?
Roses are bred
so I could express my love true
And yet, I choose bread
to appease my soul that withdrew
Roses are red?
life's askew
I'll let the cynicism shred
merci beaucoup
