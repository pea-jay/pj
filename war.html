War: What is it good for?

Inexperienced and naive, a person
writes words about war.
What is this trivial and trifling tribulation of a person
who watches from afar?
We are happy in our decision
while others turn to blood and gore.
We bask in the sun
and abhor the war. Encore!
It's tumult, it's commotion
video, propaganda, and war.
News may be mis-information
driven by man's ego we deplore.
Let's eliminate all noise and misinformation
to get to the cause deeper.
Let's get to the bigger question
as we succumb to distress and despair:
"Are all humans equal or are some humans more equal?"
